Here are some reviews from my tour of Seattle coffee shops, written with scientific precision. The results have a sample size of 1, and I am a highly untrained sommelier. The main reason for my circuit was to expand my tea hobby into a general hot drinks interest. Or, maybe to combine with my bouldering excursions to round out the Software Engineer experience.

I've opted not to include prices. Given I won't be keeping the prices up-to-date, recording how much I paid would only set up visitors for sticker shock, or perhaps spawn wistful "back in my day"'s. Wasn't that a believable explanation? In truth, that ascribes more foresight to past me than is fair; I only decided to release these as reviews for the web-dev practice. The inspiration anyone might glean from this caffeinated navel-gazing is a happy side effect!

For the historically inclined, when I visited Seattle in 2023, the average espresso would set you back $4. If I had to speculate, when you are reading this, the price of espresso is much higher per liter than gas. Tastier, too.

Enough rambling! If you've made it this far, click the reviews link to see my (in)complete list of (mostly) Seattle espresso reviews.
