Here are some reviews from my tour of Seattle coffee shops, written with scientific precision. The results have a sample size of 1, and I am a highly untrained sommelier. The main reason for my circuit was to expand my tea hobby into a general hot drinks interest. It combined with my bouldering excursions to round out the stereotypical Software Engineer experience.

I've opted not to include prices. Given I won't be keeping the prices up-to-date, recording how much I paid would only set up visitors for sticker shock, or perhaps spawn wistful "back in my day"'s. Wasn't that a believable explanation? That ascribes more foresight to past me than is fair; I only decided to release these as reviews for the web-dev practice. Any inspiration one might gain from this caffeinated navel-gazing is a happy side effect!

When I visited Seattle in 2023 the average espresso would set you back $4. If I had to speculate, the present price of espresso is much higher per liter than gas. Tastier, too.

Enough rambling! If you've made it this far, click the reviews link to see my (in)complete list of (mostly) Seattle espresso reviews.
